Course Name

Diploma in Buddhism

Course Content

  • Early Buddhism - Basic Doctrines
  • Buddhist Social Dimensions
  • Geographical Expansion of Theravada Buddhism
  • Early History of Buddhism up to 3rd Century B.C.
  • Pali Qualifying Paper (Optional)

    • Eligibility

    Above 18 years of age. Ability to read and write English. Some basic knowledge of Buddhism would be an advantage.

    Fees

  • ¥5,700 - Registration Fees - Buddhist and Pali University, Sri Lanka (in USD only)
  • ¥18.720 - Maintenance Fees - BPCHT
  • ¥10,400 - Examination Fees - Buddhist and Pali University, Sri Lanka (in USD only)

    • Registration Date

    1 Aug to 30 Sep

    How To register

    Required documents:
  • Application form (Click here to download the Application)
  • CV/Personal resume *Free format, 1-3 sheets of A4 paper
  • Copy of receipt of the application fee
  • Passport copy (non-Japanese applicants only)
  • Residence card copy [both sides] (non-Japanese applicants residing in Japan only)
  • Other education certificates

    • After completing the required documents, send them into the following address in register mail.
    Buddhist and Pali College of Hachioji, Tokyo
    C/o Sakamuni International Buddhist Center
    Shouzanji 1693-2 Yamatamachi, Hachioji-shi, Tokyo 193-0933

    Remarks

    Successful candidates are eligible to proceed to the 3-year Bachelor of Arts (BA) and then the 1-year Master of Arts (MA) in Buddhist Studies.
